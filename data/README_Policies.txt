Policy Archive for the paper
"Learning step-size adaptation in CMA-ES"

-LTO_Data (Contains the saved policies from the runs of CMA-ES with the learned policies for step-size adaptation)
-	-Sampling_Rate_Ablation (Contains the saved learned policies with sampling rate 0-0.9)
-	-	-Sampling 0.X (X = {0,1,...,9})
-	-Train_5-30D_to_35-60D (Contains the saved learned policies trained on functions of 5-30D)
-	-Transfer_Other_Fcns (Contains the saved learned policies trained on 10 BBOB functions)
